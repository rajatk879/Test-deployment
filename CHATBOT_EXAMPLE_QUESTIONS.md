## üí° Tips for Better Queries

1. **Be specific**: Include dates, mill IDs (M1, M2, M3), recipe IDs (R1-R5), or SKU IDs (SKU001-SKU014)
2. **Use time periods**: "this month", "last week", "in 2020", "January 2020"
3. **Ask for comparisons**: "compare", "show me all", "which has the highest"
4. **Request aggregations**: "total", "sum", "average", "by mill", "by recipe"
5. **Combine concepts**: "Show me mill utilization for Superior flour production"
